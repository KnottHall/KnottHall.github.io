Knottlight

Knott Hall will periodically spotlight (Knottlight!) members of its community which it is proud of for their achievements in life and at Notre Dame.

Los Presidentes

Bobby Greaser (right) and Thomas Piccione (left) are Knott Hall’s presidential leadership for the upcoming academic year. Bobby was born in Cleveland but lives in Indianapolis, whereas Thomas is from better Cleveland, AKA Pittsburgh. Both students are sophomores in the Mendoza College of Business. Thomas plans to major in IT management and psychology, while Bobby intends to major in finance and economics. They both live in the same quad, room 303 of section 3A. Bobby and Thomas are both avid sports fans, with Bobby cheering for Cleveland teams and Thomas for Pittsburgh teams. They ran for the presidency under the campaign slogan “Keep Making Knott Great Again”, and that is what they intend to do over the coming months. If you would like to help them, you can come to Hall Gov in the Chapel Lounge any Tuesday at 10:30 PM.
