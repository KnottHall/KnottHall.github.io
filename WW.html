Welcome Weekend

What to expect

All of the events on the official Welcome Weekend itinerary such as academic meetings and advice sessions are important, but ultimately the most important part of your Welcome Weekend experience will be meeting the people you’ll be living with for the coming years. Expect to be overwhelmed by the influx of names that will enter your mind and don’t worry if you forget some. Expect to meet people that in a few months you’ll consider some of your best friends. Expect your mom to cry. A lot of them do. Expect friendly upperclassmen that will be more than happy to answer any question you may have. In the end, expect that the original awkwardness will turn into brotherhood. Expect that you won’t want to go home for Fall Break. You made the right choice in choosing Notre Dame, but by the grace of God you got put in the best dorm on campus.
